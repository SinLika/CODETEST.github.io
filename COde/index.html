Sagittarius

Reading for August 14th 2023

What happens when we count our blessings and then multiply them by ten? We give our blessings the power to grow manifold, Sagittarius. What happens when we repeat the story of rejection and failure? We end up manifesting circumstances that hinder our growth and keep us from showing up the grandest version of our lives. Could this be true in your case too? Are you subconsciously giving more power to the things that aren’t working for you? It’s time to take a long, hard look in the mirror, beautiful. It’s time to pluck the weeds out of your garden, one by one.
